More professional info
I am a social communicator and journalist, professional, with a double degree in Advertising.

I have experience in designing and implementing internal communication plans, external and digital. Since my processes as a researcher and brand strategist, I have participated in the conceptualization and implementation of corporate strategies of communication and worked from the objective of contributing to the achievement of the brand’s goals.

As a communicator and advertiser, I have knowledge of brand building, image building, and strategies for building a positive reputation and building relationships of trust with target audiences. My experience in the field has allowed me to acquire writing skills; my passion for research has led me to work on the continuous search for information and trends to transform ideas into solutions.

Within my work experience, I have served as a journalist at the News Agency from the UPB, I have served as a publicist and campaign strategist for the Regional Consulting Program of the Chamber of Commerce of Medellín with the UPB, in the area of digital marketing, copying, and content creation.

I volunteered for the Smiles Foundation, in the position of digital communicator for hours, where I was in charge of tactics of digital communication, the development of the Foundation’s Corporate Identity Manual, and the design of parts for social networks.
